On Mar18 2018, Yuli Cai went on a G train as she normal does and pulled out a new app she developed to augement her Okcupid experience.
Inspired by the DTF poster from Okcupid.

Swiping to Okcupid DTF poster, AR dating on the train >>>

moving images ↓↓↓
Growing up I have always wondered how it feels like to be with the person I like. I would try to place their photo next to mine and ask my friend: "do we look like a couple?" So I made this project to answer this question.

How does it work? The demo app was made with WebAR. It detects the surroundings to see if there are Okcupid posters with the camera on my phone. If the posters were detected, I can start placing my face and the face from the profile I am swiping through in space. The 2 faces of us will be mapped onto the faces of that couple on the posters.

The difference between doing it on subway and doing it at home with Okcupid app is that the swiping experience ends with the announcement of MTA: This is the last stop of this train. Dating app can get very addictive sometimes, doing it on the subway can at least put a physical stop on it.
